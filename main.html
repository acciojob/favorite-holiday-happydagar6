Manali


Manali is a beautiful hill station located in the Himalayas, known for its scenic mountains, pleasant weather, adventure activities, and peaceful environment. It is a perfect destination for nature lovers and travelers seeking relaxation and excitement.

Top 5 Attractions

  1. Solang Valley – Famous for adventure sports and snow activities.
  2. Rohtang Pass – Known for its breathtaking views and snowfall.
  3. Hadimba Temple – A historic wooden temple surrounded by cedar forests.
  4. Old Manali – Popular for its peaceful vibes and cafes.
  5. Vashisht Hot Springs – Known for natural hot water baths.

Must-Try Foods

  • Siddu – A traditional Himachali steamed bread.
  • Thukpa – A hot noodle soup perfect for cold weather.
  • Trout Fish – Fresh and locally prepared delicacy.
  • Babru – A crispy Himachali snack.
  • Mittha – A sweet dish made with rice and dry fruits.
